By Maddie Hill


After exploring our roots, surviving our darkest hours, and celebrating our biggest wins, it's time to look ahead. What's next for Gumroad? Where do we go from here?

Throughout this series, I've emphasized how our greatest successes have come from listening to creators and building what they truly need—not chasing venture capital expectations. This creator-first philosophy continues to guide our future.

The next chapter of Gumroad is perhaps our most ambitious yet: we're going to open source the platform.

Yes, you read that correctly. We believe the tools that empower creators should be shaped by creators themselves. By opening our codebase, we're giving our community the opportunity to directly influence the platform's development, fix issues that matter to them, and build the features they need most. This isn't just about transparency—it's about giving creators a genuine stake in the tools that power their livelihoods.

But open-sourcing is just one part of our vision. Here's what else we're building in 2025:

Faster access to your money

We know that cash flow matters. That's why we're introducing instant payouts for balances over $10,000, expanding our payment methods through enhanced Stripe integration, and adding installment plans for high-value products. Your money shouldn't be locked away when you need it most.

A more powerful storefront

Your products deserve the best possible showcase. We're adding social proof widgets, testimonial capabilities (including video!), and audio previews within product descriptions. Our new Gum.new landing page builder will make creating conversion-optimized pages simpler than ever. Plus, we're completely revamping the product editing experience to give you more control over your work's presentation.

Smarter insights and marketing

Understanding your audience and sales patterns shouldn't require a data science degree. Our enhanced analytics dashboard will feature customizable charts, daily data views, and significantly faster performance. We're also adding UTM link tracking for better campaign analysis and expanding email marketing tools with single-use discount codes and batch creation options.

Better mobile experience and community building

Your business doesn't stop when you're away from your desk. Our iOS and Android apps are getting product editing and customer management capabilities. And because community matters, we're adding group chat functionality and enhanced support tools to help you engage with your customers.

Content creator enhancements

For those of you creating podcasts and courses, we're building specialized hosting and delivery tools to make your lives easier. Auto-save during product editing will ensure you never lose your work, and our enhanced recommendation algorithm will help new customers discover your creations.


Underpinning all these improvements is a technical overhaul with Next.js implementation for better performance—part of our commitment to building a platform that's both powerful and reliable.

What strikes me most as I reflect on Gumroad's journey is how our biggest breakthroughs came when we stopped trying to be everything to everyone and instead focused intently on serving our core community. That moment when we shifted from chasing hypergrowth to building sustainable value marked a turning point in our story.

As we move forward with this roadmap, we remain committed to the same principle that saved us: independence. We're not building for investors; we're building for you—the creators who use Gumroad every day to share your work with the world.

Your success is our success. The future of Gumroad isn't about becoming a billion-dollar unicorn—it's about being the best possible platform for creators to thrive on their own terms. And we think that's a much more exciting destination.

Thank you for joining me on this trip down Gumroad's history. Here's to the road ahead!
