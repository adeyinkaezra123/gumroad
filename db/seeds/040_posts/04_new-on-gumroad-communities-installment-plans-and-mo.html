Communities on Gumroad

Your digital products can now become social hubs for your customers with our new Community feature that lets customers connect with each other directly within Gumroad.

What you can do:

  • Create dedicated spaces for each product
  • Customers automatically get access when they purchase
  • Build stronger connections between customers
  • Keep conversations centered around your content
  • Reduce churn by fostering community engagement

Visit each of your product’s settings to enable this feature. Your customers will then see a prompt to join the community for any products they’ve purchased.

We’ve also shipped

  • Export multiple payouts - download all your exports at once, for those of you who have to do some serious accounting
  • Testimonials - add customer testimonials to your product descriptions
  • Installment plans - make premium products more accessible
